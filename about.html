Who Am I?

I am a professional Maths and Physics Tutor with 30 years of experience. I live in Wollaton, Nottingham, just 3 minutes walk from 2 NCT bus routes. I have helped over 1300 children with Maths, Science, and Physics. I specialize in exam preparation and have excellent references and feedback from many satisfied parents. I have also marked exam papers and can guide students to the essential key points to gain maximum marks.

My Experience

With 30+ years of tutoring experience and a Masters degree in Electronic and Electrical Engineering from Loughborough University, I bring both academic expertise and practical teaching strategies to every session. I’ve worked with a wide range of students, each with unique goals and learning styles. I tutor online and in person from Primary, KS3, GCSE and A-level and also have experience with Adult learners and children with SEN. I use Zoom/Skype and have a Graphics tablet/pen allowing me to write onto the screen and share screens. I send any notes, summaries or questions done on the screen at the end. I have over 100 online textbooks/workbooks and digital lessons which I share online or by email. I am very experienced and fluent with using online portals such as MyMaths, SPARX & MathsWatch and know how to use them effectively to their full potential to aid progress and learning. I use online quizzes and tests to check understanding and make lessons interactive. Many of my students continue with sessions for 5 or 6 years along with their siblings. Nearly all of my students improve by at least 2 or 3 grades from their starting position. I cover all the main exam boards including AQA, Edexcel and OCR.
